House of Candy™ Importers & Distributors is a family owned business established in 2017, with the goal of providing top quality confectionery products to the South African consumers.  We currently import and distribute items such as sweets, biscuits, chocolates House of Candy is currently the exclusive import agents for Gum-Bo Ballz, Bebeto, Zed Candy, Oatfield Confectionery, Goudas Gilde and King brands in South Africa.

 

We currently operate from three branches based in Cape Town, Johannesburg and Durban, and service areas across South Africa.  Our client base consists of retailers, wholesalers, delicatessens, coffee shops, pharmacies, schools, hotels, forecourts and various independent stores.  We currently also supply neighbouring countries like Namibia, Zimbabwe, Swaziland & Mozambique.

 

Creative Sweets is proud to announce our exclusive brand of top quality jelly products called King. After observing market trends over a long period of time the decision was made to launch King in 2014. After the success of the King Jelly Range, in 2016 the King range was expanded to include various novelty toys and candies. 

 

Throughout the years, Creative Sweets has grown to be known as a player in the confectionery market with a competitive edge, as well as being a company that is both reputable & reliable.  We strive to import & distribute premium local & international brands into the retail sector, and are constantly observing market trends. 

 

To this day we still adhere to the same principles as we first did back in 2002: Low Price, Great Service and the Customer Comes First attitude!

 

Should you need any further information, please feel free to contact either one of our branches and we will try to assist you with your requirements as best we can.
